Welcome to Copper Heart.

Copper Heart makes patronage easy. It's a way for communities to support people doing good work.

Background.

We all know musicians, artists, organizers, programmers or writers. And we want to help, to contribute to their cause.

Copper Heart is a way to make monthly contributions to people you know, using your credit card.

About 97 percent of each contribution goes directly to the person you're supporting. The other 3 percent goes to Stripe, for handling confidential data and processing cards.

Anyone in the United States or Canada can contribute.

Made in Oregon, USA.

Copper Heart is influenced by the work of Jonathan Haidt and his moral foundations theory, and by the Heath brothers and their work in psychology.

Kickstarter accentuates our societal moment of crowd funding, with Stripe and Nodejitsu making services to give projects like these a place to stand, joining the chorus of Gumroad, Gittip, and Bandcamp.

This engineering work is made possible by the freely available Node.js, express, Apache CouchDB, AngularJS, jQuery, GitHub, and many communities like the Open Source Initiative, who have created a wealth of tools for the benefit of all humanity.

This is a secret project, inspired by the Corvallis Swing Dance Society.

Created in Corvallis, Oregon.

Create your own.

You can set up your own Copper Heart in about a day. The code for this project is freely available on GitHub.
